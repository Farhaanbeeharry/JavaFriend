Your Java Friend

Your Java friend is a helping tool which can teach you and guide you through the steps to learn and program in Java. You can use this application by either typing in
the box below or by using voice commands. You can ask the system whatever you want to know about Java. Here are some examples: "What is Java?", "Why
use Java?", "Open NetBeans website", "Help me setup my environment", "What are constructors?", "More on characters", "What is regular expressions?" and "Teach me hello world".
You can ask for 'help', 'clear the conversation' and 'clear the conversation and start over again'. You can also have fun! Try asking me "change your name to stupid", "reset your name" or "Koi Mil Gaya"...

Hello, this is your Java Friend. How can I help you?

Press ENTER to send command or use voice button on the right to speak
